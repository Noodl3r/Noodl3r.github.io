Freshman Year, Obsidian and MathJax

Being my first year of college I took this year quite easy academically, rather than skipping past all the courses I had AP credit for, I chose to retake intro physics and Calc 2 due to having self studied these courses in high school. I did however chose to skip the intro gen chem sequence as I was not premed and had competed in Chemistry Olympiads through high school.

This summer I also had my first research job. I worked on CUDA simulations of Xenon scintillation. It was incredibly interesting and fulfilling and motivated my interest in future employment in physics.
